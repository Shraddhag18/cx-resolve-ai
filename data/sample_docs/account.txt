Account Management Guide

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You will receive a reset link within 5 minutes. The link expires after 1 hour. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

How do I change my email address?
Go to Account Settings > Profile > Email Address. Enter your new email and click Save. A verification link will be sent to the new address. Your email will not change until you click the verification link.

How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?
Two-factor authentication can be enabled under Account Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS. We recommend using an authenticator app for stronger security.

How do I delete my account?
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. To delete your account, go to Account Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. You will be asked to confirm by typing your email address. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

How do I transfer my account to another person?
Account transfers are not supported directly. The new owner must create their own account and you can invite them to your workspace. Contact support if you need to transfer a paid subscription.

What do I do if my account was hacked?
Immediately reset your password and revoke all active sessions under Account Settings > Security > Active Sessions. Enable 2FA if not already active. Contact support to report the incident and audit recent activity.

How many team members can I invite?
The number of team members depends on your plan. Starter plans allow up to 5 members. Pro plans allow up to 25 members. Enterprise plans have unlimited members. Go to Account Settings > Team to manage invitations.
